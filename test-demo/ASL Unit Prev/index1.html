UNIT PREVIEW

In this unit, we will explore the historical, cultural, technological, and legal dimensions of Esports. We will uncover the origins of competitive gaming, analyze how Esports is influenced by outside factors, and explore the various gaming platforms available. We will also work to understand the challenges of fostering an inclusive Esports culture, as well as unravel the legal ramifications of policies and legislation related to the Esports industry.

Essential Questions

  • Explore the history of Esports and identify key stakeholders and influencers. (ES-5.2)
  • Analyze the corresponding influence of Esports, technology, media, and culture. (ES-5.1)
  • Identify gaming platform(s) and identify factors contributing to the platform’s success or failure. (ES-5.3)
  • Identify the challenges to creating an inclusive culture in Esports and potential barriers that exist in the ecosystem.
  • Explain the legal ramifications of policies like The Digital Millennum Copyright Act (DMCA) and how legislation
